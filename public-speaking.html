PUBLIC SPEAKING

Conflict is a topic that everyone can relate to and everyone needs to know about. Yet conflict is often viewed as a difficult or uncomfortable subject when it arises.

CCR International's speakers address the topic of conflict in a matter-of-fact, forthright manner that is, at the same time, entertaining and engaging. CCR International personnel are frequent speakers at conferences and conventions on a wide variety of topics related to conflict in the workplace.

Some of our most popular presentations include:

  • Conflict is for the Birds!
  • Building Functional Harmony in Your Workplace
  • Fighting Fires without Getting Burnt
  • Negotiating for Results
  • Navigating the Minefield of Conflict in the Workplace: A Case Study
  • Embracing and Leading Change

INTERVENTIONS

But for the individuals involved it may be extremely difficult to find their way out of the situation on their own. The assistance of a neutral third party will allow the involved individuals to talk together in a safe and controlled environment, focusing on the key issues, acknowledging points of agreement, and exploring opportunities for moving forward.

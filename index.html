Conflict Can Be a Positive Force in Your Organization.

Conflict, simply put, is a difference or gap. It can be a difference of wants, needs, or expectations. A gap between perceptions and intentions, intended outcomes and actual outcomes.

In its best forms, conflict is a catalyst for dialogue and creativity. Successful companies know this and learn to manage these gaps effectively, turning conflict into an opportunity for growth and change.

For over 20 years Centre for Conflict Resolution International has been partnering with organizations to bridge the gap between chaotic, destructive conflict and engaging, creative functionality.

We have developed and utilize a variety of tools, processes and resources that allow people and organizations to resolve issues that are negatively impacting them and to restore “functional harmony” in the workplace.

Please explore our website to find out more about us and how we can help. Or contact us by phone or email to begin the conversation.

The perspective of a third party can prove invaluable in solving these problems. Because of our vast experience in dealing with workplace conflict issues, CCR International Conflict Management Specialists are available to consult with you, assisting you in identifying issues and determining appropriate processes for addressing those issues so that you can tackle the situation and find solutions. We will work with you in an informal manner that is collaborative and non-threatening.

COACHING

Coaching is a self-reflective, focused and meaningful conversation. Coaching allows the individual to explore the assumptions, beliefs, attitudes, and behaviors that are getting in the way of achieving his/her goals â€“ whether that is to be an effective leader, to resolve conflict, to work more effectively with others, or to live life in a positive and intentional way.

CCR offers executive/leadership coaching, development coaching, as well as crisis or conflict just-in-time coaching.

The goals of coaching are:

  • To discover and clarify the personal and professional goals that matter deeply to you
  • To understand yourself better so that you can recognize the blocks and patterns that get in your way
  • To develop solutions and strategies that will help you achieve your goals
  • To sustain positive change, infusing your life and work with purpose, passion, and a greater sense of well-being
